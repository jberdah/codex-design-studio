# Visual quality rubric

For broad redesigns, evaluate the desktop and mobile renders against these dimensions:

- **Intent:** the requested direction is immediately visible, not merely described in metadata.
- **Hierarchy:** one dominant message, clear supporting copy, and unambiguous actions.
- **Composition:** deliberate balance, alignment, rhythm, and whitespace at both viewports.
- **Typography:** readable sizes and line lengths, with no clipped or orphaned display text.
- **Interaction:** navigation labels remain accessible; icons support rather than replace meaning.
- **Brand coherence:** shared tokens remain recognizable unless the request explicitly changes them.
- **Integrity:** no horizontal overflow, missing sections, broken assets, or selection IDs lost.

Treat pixel difference as evidence of change, not evidence of quality. Inspect the rendered screenshots before reporting success.
